Aliza Smart Mirror For Autistic Children

Aliza is a system of teaching excellence for Autistic children.

Get Started Watch Video

Aliza!

Autism is a neurodevelopmental syndrome that causes social communication and behavioral challenges. People with Autism have difficulty with complex language, nonverbal communication as well as expressing their needs and emotions. Therefore, it can be challenging to teach them tasks such as reading, writing and listening. It is important to help autistic children develop their education with the help of modern technologies.

Learn More

Therefore, we present an education system which is “Aliza” smart mirror that would help autistic children in their education and learning habits. The system has the ability to

  • Teach Pre-writing Skills
  • Teach Basic Math
  • Teach Speech Training
  • Track Users Facial Reactions

Why ALIZA?

  • >>ASD students struggle with writing, speaking and communicating.

  • >>Continues monitoring is essential for ASD children because they tend to distract very easily.

  • >>The use of wall-based interactive systems such as Magic Mirror can be used to help develop screen engagement skills.
  • >>Manual therapy sessions are not affordable and accessible at the same time to everyone.

Services

Aliza provides four main services to ASD students to improve their skills.

Shapes Drawing Training

We believe ASD students will be able to learn successfully develop their prewriting skills through our writing activities such as lines, curves and shapes drawing.

Mathematical Training

We teaching typically begin with simple matching and labelling approach to understanding basic maths concepts to young children with ASD. We trust this fun activity base math teaching is to help the children to develop their math basic knowledge.

Basic Verbal Training

We provide interesting word practice games and missing letter finding game activities to ASD students. We believe these activities help to build the speaking skills very interactive.

Attentiveness Tracking

To keep learning focus of ASD students, we are always tracking their facial reaction by our ” Attentiveness tracker”. Through this tracker, motor skill activities are provided to them to enhance their concentration with fun activities.
